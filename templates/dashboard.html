🚗 Ace Smart Ride

Ace Smart Ride is an intercity ride-sharing web application that connects drivers and passengers for easy and affordable travel.

🚀 Features

• Post and book rides
• Live location tracking 🗺️
• Chat system 💬
• Driver rating system ⭐
• AI chatbot assistant 🤖

📘 How to Use (For New Users)

👤 Step 1: Register
Create an account as a Driver or Passenger.

🔐 Step 2: Login
Login using your email and password.

🚗 Step 3: Use Based on Role

For Drivers:
• Add ride details (source, destination, date, seats, price)
• Wait for passengers to book your ride
• Mark ride as completed after journey

For Passengers:
• View available rides
• Click "Book" to reserve a seat
• Contact driver after booking
• Rate the driver after ride completion ⭐

🤖 Chatbot Help:
Use the chatbot to search rides, check prices, and get help instantly.

🎯 Our goal is to make travel smart, safe, and affordable for everyone.
